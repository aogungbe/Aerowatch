We do more than track flights

Filter flights by Route, Departure Airport or Arrival Airport. All the flight data is provided by the great people at OpenSky Network and their difficult but extremely useful REST API. Their API is free, so they dont have the resources to track or get the full dataset for every flight.The OpenSky Network operates from Switzerland. It is a non-profit association based in Switzerland that collects and provides access to air traffic control and surveillance data from all over the world.
